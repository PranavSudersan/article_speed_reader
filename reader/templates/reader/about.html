Speed Reader was created as a web application for lazy readers such as myself who struggles with pushing through pages and pages of text in online articles. This app simplifies the reading process by showing you chunks of phrases in series from the article that you want to read. This continoous stream of flashing words is easily registered by our eyes simply by staring at a fixed area, significantly reducing your reading time.
